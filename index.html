ABOUT BIG OAK

The Big Oak Cafe began in 2005 as an idea to offer a place for coffee and espresso drinks while shopping at The Butcher Shoppe. It quickly turned into a cafe with a full menu that includes cold drinks, sandwiches, soups, salads, breakfast items, desserts and more. Much more than an extra stop while shopping, the Big Oak Cafe is now its own destination with seating for about 60 inside and much more outside on our porch and patio in nice weather.
